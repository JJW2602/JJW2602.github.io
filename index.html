Jongwook Jeon

I'm an undergraduate student in Yonsei University majoring in Electrical and Electronic Engineering. Currently, I am advised by Prof. Jongmin Lee.

My research interests center on building generalizable generative policies that can adapt across offline datasets, online interaction, and long-horizon real-world tasks. In particular, I focus on two directions:

  • RL Fine-tuning for Generative Policy Models:
    improving imitation-learned policies through RL fine-tuning, with a focus on flow-based and diffusion-based policy models.
  • Skill-based and Hierarchical Reinforcement Learning:
    composing reusable skills for long-horizon tasks, especially in VLA models and flow-/diffusion-based policies.

Publications

(*: indicates equal contribution)
Learning Multimodal One-step Flow Policy via Value-weighted Optimal Transport Under Review Jaehun Shon*, Jinha Choi*, Jongwook Jeon, Jongmin Lee

We introduce OptiFlow, a novel approach for learning multimodal one-step flow policies using value-weighted optimal transport, enabling more effective and efficient policy learning in complex environments.

MIMDICE: Unsupervised Skill Discovery from Arbitrary Off-policy Experience Under Review Younghyun Kim, Jongwook Jeon, Jongmin Lee

We introduce MIMDICE, an off-policy algorithm that directly optimizes the mutual information with respect to the policy's stationary distribution via density ratio correction, enabling the use of arbitrary unlabeled data without requiring skill annotations.

Projects

These include side projects and unpublished research work.

AI Pharmacy Copilot for Robotic Medication Sorting Jongwook Jeon, Juwon Kim, Hyeongjin Kim, Jinha Choi, Woonjae Cho, xyz AI x Robotics Hackathon (Grand Prize)

Motivated by the repetitive workload and fatigue pharmacists often face in sorting and organizing medications, we developed an end-to-end AI robotic system that combines speech understanding, RGB-D perception, segmentation, VLM-based planning, and ROS2 control with safety and recovery mechanisms.

Optimizing Optimization and Learning Rate Scheduler Jongwook Jeon, Subin Park, Seungwoo Kang, Sihyeon Park

By modeling the optimizer and learning rate scheduler updates as a sequential decision process, we optimize the strategy via a combination of BC and PPO.

FinPT: Finance Chatbot Jongwook Jeon*, Minkyung Baek, Kyunghwan Kim Yonsei 2nd GenAI (SilverPrize)

Motivated by the critical issue of financial illiteracy among college students, FinPT is a web service designed to make financial knowledge accessible and user-friendly. By leveraging RAG technology, FinPT retrieves real-time data to provide students with up-to-date and easy-to-understand financial information.
